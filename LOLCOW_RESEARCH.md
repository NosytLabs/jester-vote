# Lolcow Research & Definition

## What is a Lolcow?

### Definition
A **lolcow** (also spelled "lol cow") is a person who is exploited and mocked by others online for their eccentric, foolish, or dysfunctional behavior. The term combines "LOL" (laugh out loud) with "cow" (suggesting the person is "milked" for entertainment).

### Origin
- **First documented use:** Early 2000s on 4chan and Something Awful forums
- **Popularized by:** Encyclopedia Dramatica (ED) and Kiwi Farms
- **Etymology:** The person is "milked" for laughs - hence "cow"

### Key Characteristics of a TRUE Lolcow

1. **Unawareness** - They don't realize they're being laughed AT, not with
2. **Consistency** - They consistently provide entertainment through dysfunction
3. **Exploitability** - Easy to provoke into reactions
4. **Documentation** - Extensive archives of their behavior exist
5. **Community** - Dedicated communities form around documenting them
6. **Resistance to Change** - They never learn from mistakes
7. **Drama Generation** - Constant chaos and controversy

### Types of Lolcows

**1. Gaming Lolcows**
- Blame external factors for failures
- Rage publicly
- Can't accept criticism
- Examples: DSP, Wings of Redemption, LowTierGod

**2. IRL Lolcows**
- Document their dysfunctional lives publicly
- Mental health issues broadcast
- Self-destructive behavior
- Examples: Chris Chan, KingCobraJFS, OnlyUseMeBlade

**3. E-Celeb Lolcows**
- Started as normal content creators
- Fame revealed dysfunction
- Scandals and downfalls
- Examples: Boogie2988, Projared, Onision

**4. Political/Commentary Lolcows**
- Extreme views expressed publicly
- Constant debates and drama
- Platform bans
- Examples: Andrew Tate, Sneako
